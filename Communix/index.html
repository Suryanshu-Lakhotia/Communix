About Us

Communix aims to be the single communication platform for everyone by incorporating features so as to ease communication for deaf and mute. We also plan to extend support to multinational companies, businesses and organizations by assisting them in making notes in meeting.

Unable to hear? Can't Speak? Need help in making notes? Communix has your back!

Hassle Free Communication, from the comfort of your doorstep!

  • We translate sign language and gestures made by mute people into text so as to make them understandable for other people.
  • Every day thousands of MNCs, businesses and organizations around the globe face problems in recording important notes in meeting. Communix can convert live spoken sentences to text with the timestamps and speakers name, thus giving an easy and readable acces to the user.
  • Deaf people can communicate more easily now with our live captioning feature in video calls.

Objectives

Our objective is to contribute in the improvement of e-accessibility for people with disabilitites and to make the cell phone an easy way of communication for them. We also aim to scale up by providing our meeting notes generation feature to businesses and organizations.

A Comfortable Alternative!

Messengers and texting, as famous developer Wyhowski once said, are slightly better, but they do not solve the problem, which is translation, and do not offer an easy, confident and comfortable way to communicate.
